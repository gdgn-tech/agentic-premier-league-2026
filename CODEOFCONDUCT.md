# Code of Conduct

# Agentic Premier League 2026 🚀
### Powered by Antigravity

The Agentic Premier League is committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, collaborative, and respectful environment for all participants.

This includes:
- Participants
- Mentors
- Organizers
- Judges
- Volunteers
- Sponsors
- Community members

By participating in this hackathon, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.

---

# Our Mission

We aim to:
- Encourage innovation and learning
- Promote collaboration and open-source culture
- Build impactful AI products responsibly
- Foster a safe and inclusive developer community

---

# Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

✅ Be respectful and professional  
✅ Support fellow participants  
✅ Encourage collaboration and learning  
✅ Give constructive feedback  
✅ Maintain ethical AI and development practices  
✅ Respect diversity of opinions and backgrounds  
✅ Follow GitHub contribution guidelines  
✅ Maintain professionalism during demos and discussions

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will NOT be tolerated:

❌ Harassment or discrimination  
❌ Hate speech or offensive comments  
❌ Personal attacks or bullying  
❌ Plagiarism or stealing projects/code without attribution  
❌ Spamming or disruptive behavior  
❌ Sharing malicious, harmful, or illegal content  
❌ Intentionally sabotaging another team’s work  
❌ Unauthorized access to systems or data  
❌ Creating unsafe or unethical AI systems

---

# AI & Open Source Ethics

Participants must ensure:

- Responsible use of AI tools and APIs
- Proper attribution for open-source libraries and assets
- No misuse of private or sensitive data
- Compliance with platform/API usage policies
- Transparent AI workflows where applicable

---

# Collaboration Rules

- Teams may learn from public resources and frameworks
- Copy-pasting full projects without meaningful contribution is prohibited
- Contributors should maintain clean commit history and documentation
- Teams should use Pull Requests and proper branching workflows

---

# GitHub Repository Standards

All teams must:

- Maintain a proper README
- Use meaningful commit messages
- Follow repository folder structure
- Avoid pushing directly to the main branch
- Keep documentation updated

---

# Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the organizers immediately.

Organizers reserve the right to:
- Issue warnings
- Remove content
- Disqualify teams
- Restrict participation if necessary

---

# Fair Play Policy

The spirit of this hackathon is:
- Learning
- Innovation
- Community
- Collaboration

Winning is important, but building responsibly and supporting the community matters more.

---

# Final Note

Build products that solve real problems.
Support your teammates.
Respect the community.
Create responsibly.

Let’s build the future of Agentic AI together 🚀
